If you struggle to find an answer below, get in touch.

You can email us at help@kind.co.uk or join our Facebook group.

General

Can I contact anybody at KIND?

If you have any questions about the KIND website please email help@kind.co.uk

You can also reach out on Twitter and Facebook

Should I be self isolating? What are the symptoms of coronavirus?

KIND is run by volunteers.

We are not medical professionals and we are not qualified to give medical advice.

Please visit the NHS website for health information and advice.

Can I get involved with KIND?

Absolutely! We're a group of volunteers and welcome offers of support.

Please email help@kind.co.uk for more information.

If you're a developer please head over to our public GitHub repository to get involved.

Offering Help

I don't want to give my contact number to strangers.

Unfortunately the only way to offer help via KIND is by listing your mobile number.

This is the most direct and clear way to communicate in these urgent times of need.

Can I stipulate the terms of my help?

You can outline the help you are willing to provide (for example, the times and type of help you can offer). The more information you add, the better.

Can I edit or remove my offer of help if my situation changes?

Please email help@kind.co.uk with your mobile number and we will edit or remove your offer of help ASAP.

How will I know if those asking for help are actually self isolating?

We are relying on communication between both parties to be honest, open and responsible.

If you have concerns please email help@kind.co.uk.

Will my information be visible publicly?

Yes. However, we only ask for your first name, mobile number and first part of your postcode. Your offer of help will only appear on searches that match your postcode.

What do I do if too many people contact me?

If demand for your help is becoming overwhelming, please consider removing your details from KIND by emailing help@kind.co.uk.

Data Protection

What data do you store?

If you choose to register as a local hero, KIND will store your name, postcode, mobile number and offer of help.

This information will be publicly visible to anyone searching in your postcode area.

Will you sell my data or pass it on to third-parties?

No. We will never sell or transfer your data.

However, all information submitted will be publicly visible.

How can I remove my details from KIND

Please email help@kind.co.uk with your mobile number and we will remove your details ASAP.

How do I report abuse?

Please email help@kind.co.uk with information and we will get back to you ASAP.
